No Poverty

End poverty in all its forms everywhere and ensure dignity for all.

Why SDG 1 Matters

Poverty remains one of the most significant challenges worldwide, affecting access to education, healthcare, and basic human needs. Sustainable Development Goal 1 focuses on reducing poverty through inclusive economic growth, social protection systems, and equal opportunities for vulnerable communities.
